Bitborbit (BBBT)

A serious Bitcoin-inspired digital asset built on scarcity, simplicity, and transparency.

What is Bitborbit?

Bitborbit is a fixed-supply cryptocurrency inspired by Bitcoin’s economic philosophy. With a total supply capped at 21 million tokens, Bitborbit is designed as a long-term store-of-value asset using modern blockchain infrastructure.

Core Principles

Scarcity

Fixed supply of 21,000,000 BBBT. No inflation. No future minting.

Simplicity

Minimal smart contract with no hidden mechanics or complex logic.

Transparency

Publicly verified contract and fully auditable token supply.

Burn Mechanism

Optional token burning to further increase scarcity over time.
